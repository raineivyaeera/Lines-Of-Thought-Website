Visual Arts

I've always been an artist. From when I was 8 drawing little monsters in my notebook, 2D animation when I was 11, drawing in MS Paint, to generative art using P5.js, Blender 3D modeling, pixel art for games, and Krita abstract art. More include photography, illustration, digital manipulation of screenshots, collages, etc. Art is very integral to my process and always has been. From the cover art I use on my albums, article covers on the writing I do, or just getting my idea across, it's very vital to my core as a creative. Often feeds back into article ideas, improvement of routines, music concepts, and things I want to talk about & work on.
