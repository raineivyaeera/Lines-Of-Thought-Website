Mastering

I was super happy to have done this master for my friend Nemo. Absolutely stunning song, gives me chills every time, and I pin it happily as a definitive work in my mastering portfolio.

Amy is a talented pianist, composer & producer with a very special asthetic in her music. Proud to have mastered most of the tracks on this release (and provided a little sound design to boot!), check out these lovely tunes.

Lena and I have made music together and have been collaborators for years now. When I heard they were doing a slowcore release, I wanted to offer my help mastering, and so this was my first mastering job other than my own music. I took my time on it, learning many fundamentals that I breezed by before, and truly learned a lot from it. Still proud of this and the attention to detail I gave it. Beautiful record.
