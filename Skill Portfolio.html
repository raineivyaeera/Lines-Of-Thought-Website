Personality & Ethics

1. Value is peer to peer, and I do not care about corporate settings. I'm fully myself, to filter out anyone not worth my time & care. I am energetic, caring, insightful, and absurd!

2. I'm the kind of person to put my entire soul, love, and attention into a project, doing much more work than is asked for, and often dedicating 8 - 16 hours a day to pure focused work.

3. Minimalist & Essentialist: all of what I own fits in an average vehicle. I sleep on the floor (with a blanket) by choice. I'm happy with very little, eat once a day, no refined sugars or bread.

4. Nothing exists in a vaccum. I have lots of other skills like photography, abstract art, cooking, writing, 3D modeling, pixel art, website dev (the website you're viewing & this one), programming, game dev, etc. All of these feed back into eachother, giving different insights and unique ideas.

5. Anyone is capable of what I do, if they are set on it. I'm not above or below anyone. I've just put in the time.


Services & Rates

My rates are very accomodating and highly dependant on the project and services required. For a song I go per minute at 20 - 30$ (depends on complexity).

For sound effects/general sound engineering, I go 20$ an hour.

I master everything I do, with no attatched price, but it is under a pay what you feel! Mastering is absorbetently expensive and I'm glad to remove that burden.

At the end of the day, I'm happy to work within people's price range if I love the project & person. If any of this seems appealing or you want to reach out and talk, I very much welcome it and my contact info is back on the home page!~
